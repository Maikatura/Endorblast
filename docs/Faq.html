Game Questions

What is Endorblast?

Endorblast is a 2D Online ARPG Side scroller game...... What else do we need to say?

Are Endorblast Open Source?

Yes, But public repo is a older build of the game to prevent more cheating and exploits
and don't have the sprites or sort of assets to prevent people to just people making copies of our game.

But of course if people wanna make a copy of Endorblast they need to pay us a royalties to be able to use it.

Will Endorblast be free at launch?

Yes.....

Endorblast will be free to play as it looks at the current state.

Will Endorblast have AddOns support?

Yes, But not at the start of the launch.

There are more important stuff to focus on then AddOns support in the start but a
exampels of addons player will be able to do is using our api that we will do at a later date
will be changing UI, Fonts and if someone makes a HD remake of all items it well be able to rescale
everything to work perfectly with the HD textures instead of the pixel art.

Economy Questions

How will Endorblast make money if its not Pay To Win?

We that are working on Endorblast DON'T want to make a game where you will be able to pay your way to the top in form of in game items and buffs. Well it will have a store but not for that reason. Well other MMORPGs have no pay to win but in other form cosmetic items and that will our store be for, Cosmetic Items only.

In the store you will be able to buy clothes, pets and other cosmetic items that will NEVER effect your gameplay.
If that system for cosmetic items is good we will of course add more content to that store like limited time items.

But we will never just focus on items for the store to make money because content in the game is more important to make the game fun to play.
